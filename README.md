# CPA Math Micro-Animations (CPA 数学微动画)

![Education](https://img.shields.io/badge/Curriculum-Singapore%20MOE-blue)
![Audience](https://img.shields.io/badge/Audience-Primary%201--6-orange)
![Interface](https://img.shields.io/badge/Interface-Interactive-green)

An interactive, visual learning platform covering **26 key mathematical concepts** from the Singapore Primary School (P1-P6) curriculum, designed using the **CPA (Concrete-Pictorial-Abstract)** approach.

---

## 🌟 Project Overview | 项目简介

This platform is a specialized educational tool designed to bridge the gap between abstract mathematical theories and students' understanding. By using dynamic micro-animations, we transform complex word problems and concepts into intuitive, interactive visual experiences.

本项目是一个专门的教学工具，旨在架起抽象数学理论与学生理解之间的桥梁。通过动态微动画，我们将复杂的应用题和数学概念转化为直观、可交互的视觉体验。

---

## 🚀 Key Features | 核心功能

- **Pedagogical CPA Method**: Follows the Ministry of Education's preferred strategy—transitioning from concrete visuals to pictorial representations and finally abstract mathematical symbols.
- **Grade-Specific Modules**: Tailored content for every stage of primary education (P1 to P6).
- **Multilingual Experience**: Seamlessly switch between English, Chinese, or Bilingual modes to support diverse language backgrounds.
- **Interactive Demonstrations**: Students and teachers can control the flow of animations to explore concepts at their own pace.
- **Universal Accessibility**: Optimized for use on desktops, tablets, and interactive whiteboards in classrooms.

## 📚 Curriculum Map | 课程大纲

### Primary 1 - 2 (Foundation)
- **Numbers & Operations**: Addition & Subtraction (100以内加减法)
- **Measurement**: Length & Time (长度与时间)
- **Basic Logic**: Multiplication & Division (乘法与除法)
- **Practical Math**: Money & Volume (金钱与体积)
- **Visual Fractions**: Introduction to Fractions (分数初步认识)

### Primary 3 - 4 (Intermediate)
- **Fractions Mastery**: Equivalent Fractions, Mixed Numbers & Improper Fractions (等值分数、带分数与假分数)
- **Geometry & Space**: Area & Perimeter, Angles & Lines, Symmetry (面积与周长、角与线、对称)
- **Advanced Numbers**: Decimals, Whole Numbers & Rounding (小数、大数与四舍五入)
- **Time Management**: Time Duration & 24h Clock (时间计算)

### Primary 5 - 6 (Advanced)
- **Proportional Reasoning**: Ratio, Percentage, Rate (比例、百分比、速率)
- **Advanced Geometry**: Volume of Cuboids, Geometric Folding, Circles (体积、折叠与角度、圆)
- **Problem Solving**: The Remainder Concept, Step-charging Rates (分支法、阶梯计费)
- **Heuristics & Algebra**: Algebra, Speed, Complex Word Problems, Patterns, Simultaneous Equations (代数、速度、应用题、规律、等量代换)

## 📖 How to Use | 如何使用

1.  **Select a Topic**: Use the dashboard to find the specific mathematical concept or grade level.
2.  **Toggle Language**: Use the buttons at the top to select your preferred instruction language.
3.  **Interact**: Click buttons within the animations to trigger steps, reset visualizations, or input variables where applicable.

---

## 📝 About the CPA Approach
The **Concrete-Pictorial-Abstract (CPA)** approach is a highly effective framework for teaching math. 
1.  **Concrete**: Using visual "objects" to represent numbers.
2.  **Pictorial**: Turning objects into diagrams or bar models.
3.  **Abstract**: Connecting visuals to numbers and symbols.
