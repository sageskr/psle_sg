Introduction to Fractions

What is a Fraction?
1 out of 4 equal parts
These are NOT quarters! ❌
1 out of 4 equal parts = one quarter
1 whole
↑ Numerator (parts taken)
$$\frac{1}{4}$$
↓ Denominator (total equal parts)
Numerator = parts taken.
Denominator = total equal parts.
$$\frac{1}{4} = 1 \div 4$$
$$\frac{\text{Numerator (分子)}}{\text{Denominator (分母)}}$$
$$\frac{1}{2}$$
$$\frac{1}{3}$$
$$\frac{1}{4}$$
$$\frac{1}{5}$$
Bigger denominator → Smaller pieces!
